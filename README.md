Over 1 million crime records analyzed

Property crimes form the largest share compared to violent crimes

Crime trends show a peak during recent years, followed by a decline

Evening and late-night hours have the highest crime occurrences

Vehicle-related crimes, burglary, and battery are among the most frequent

Majority of cases are reported as incidents, with fewer resulting in arrests

Victims in the late 20s to mid-30s age group are most affected
